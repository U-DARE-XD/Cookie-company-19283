Choclate chip cookie

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour: 2 cups
  • Granulated sugar: 1 cup
  • Brown sugar: 1 cup, packed
  • Butter or margarine: 1 cup (2 sticks), softened
  • Eggs: 2
  • Vanilla extract: 1 teaspoon
  • Baking powder: 1 teaspoon
  • Baking soda: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Salt: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Chocolate chips or chunks: 1-2 cups
  • Nuts (such as walnuts, pecans, or almonds): 1 cup, chopped (optional)
  • Rolled oats (for oatmeal cookies): 2 cups

Steps & instructions

Start here

  1. Preheat the oven to the specified temperature (e.g., 350°F or 175°C).

  2. Cream together butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy.

  3. Mix in eggs one at a time, followed by vanilla extract.

  4. Gradually add and mix in dry ingredients (flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt).

  5. Optionally, add any additional ingredients like chocolate chips, nuts, or spices.

  6. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto a lined baking sheet, or shape and cut as desired.

  7. Bake for recommended time until golden brown, then cool on a wire rack before enjoying.
